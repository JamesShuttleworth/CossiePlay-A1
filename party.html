Cossieplay Parties

Our parties take place on the first Saturday of every month at the town hall from 6pm - 11pm.
Entry is $15 for adults, $10 for kids and teens (ages 6 -17). Children under 6 are free!
Each entry includes one drink (non-alcoholic) and a glow stick.
Parties feature a live DJ - Requests are taken!



Party Guidelines

Note: All party-goers are responsible for their own safety. You should check with your doctor before attending.
Party fees MUST be paid at the door. Cash or EFTPOS accepted. Credit card payments attract a 2% surcharge.
Alcohol is served at the bar to over 18s only. Intoxicated people will NOT be served, but will be escorted to a taxi paid for by the party-goer.
