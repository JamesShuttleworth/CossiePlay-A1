What is CosPlay?

Simply put, Cosplay is a performance art in which people (cosplayers) dress up in costumes in order to represent a certain character. Characters can range anywhere from TV shows, video games, comic books, anime - you name it!

Click the link below to find out more about Cosplay!

About CossiePlay

CossiePlay is a family-run business, operating in Hulkstown since 2012. We create outstanding custom-made costumes and buy and sell second-hand costumes (check out the costumes page!) as well. We also throw awesome cosplay parties on the first Saturday of every month at the town hall!


The CossiePlay Family (our photos are provided)

Sally Rogers – After only 6 weeks of her first dress-making course, Sally was already designing and sewing elaborate ball gowns and costumes that amazed her instructors. 10 years later and she’s making even more amazing creations for happy customers.
